My hometown is located in Xi'an, Shaanxi, ChinaKnown as Chang'an for most of its history, Xi'an was one of the four ancient capitals of China and played a role in several of the most important dynasties in Chinese history, including the Western Zhou, Western Han, Sui, Northern Zhou and Tang. Xi'an is now the second most popular tourist destination in China. Xi'an is also the starting point of the Silk Road and home to the Terracotta Warriors and Horses of Qin Shihuang, one of the Eight Wonders of the World, both listed as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Tourist Attractions

Xi'an is a famous cultural and tourist city in China and even in the world. Its history and cultural accumulation are extremely profound, and it is well-known at home and abroad. It is the first city called Jing in Chinese history. In history, many prosperous dynasties including Zhou, Western Han, Sui, and Tang established their capitals here. Therefore, there are a large number of ancient cultural relics such as Qinshihuang Mausoleum, Han Yang Mausoleum, Han Chang'an City Ruins, and Tang Daming Palace Ruins scattered in and around Xi'an. Xi'an has also become a famous tourist city , Terracotta Warriors, Shaanxi History Museum, Da-Yan Tower, Datang Everbright City are all famous tourist attractions in Xi'an.
